Intel Founded

Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore rename the newly formed company NM Electronics to Intel Corporation, laying the foundation for decades of technological innovation and sustainable business practices.

1971

First Microprocessor

Intel debuts the 4004, the world's first commercial microprocessor...

First Microprocessor

Intel debuts the 4004, the world's first commercial microprocessor, igniting the microprocessor revolution. This innovation would eventually lead to energy-efficient computing solutions worldwide.

1978

8086 Processor

Launch of the 8086 processor, establishing the x86 architecture...

8086 Processor

The 8086 processor establishes the x86 architecture that would drive countless PCs and servers. Intel begins implementing more efficient manufacturing processes to reduce environmental impact.

1985

386 Processor

Intel introduces the 386 processor with 32-bit architecture...

386 Processor

Intel introduces the 386 processor with 32-bit architecture, ushering in a new era of performance. The company begins formal environmental policies at its manufacturing sites.

2006

Peak GHG Emissions

This year marks Intel's highest annual greenhouse gas emissions...

Turning Point

After reaching peak emissions, Intel initiates aggressive reduction programs, investing in chemical abatement, renewable energy, and energy-efficient manufacturing processes.

2020

RISE Strategy

Intel launches its RISE strategy and 2030 goals...

RISE Strategy

Intel's RISE (Responsible, Inclusive, Sustainable, Enabling) strategy sets ambitious 2030 goals for climate action, water stewardship, and waste reduction across the semiconductor industry.

2022

Net-Zero By 2040

Intel announces its commitment to achieve net-zero...

Net-Zero Commitment

Intel pledges to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions across global operations by 2040, with interim 2030 targets for renewable energy and process improvements.

2023

Renewable Electricity

The company achieves 99% renewable electricity usage...

Renewable Milestone

Intel reaches 99% renewable electricity usage worldwide through power purchase agreements, on-site generation, and renewable energy credits.

2024

Sustainability Summit

Intel hosts its first Sustainability Summit...

Collaborative Future

The inaugural Sustainability Summit brings together suppliers, government officials, and industry leaders to accelerate sustainable semiconductor manufacturing innovations.
